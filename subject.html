Background Info

The Taking of the Pelham 123 was produced in 2009 and is a remake of a movie of the same name produced in 1974.

It stars some famous actors and actresses including John Travolta and James Gandolfini.

The movie takes place in New York City, and the name of the movie comes from the train that is being robbed in it. This train is the 6 train departing from Pelham Bay Park Station at 1:23 PM.
